Huazhong University of Science and Technology

"A bright virtue, a strong learning, a search for truth and innovation."

HUST has implemented the strategy of "talent development" and has a strong teaching staff. There are more than 3,700 full-time teachers, including more than 1,400 professors and 1,400 associate professors; 19 academicians, 15 chief scientists of the "973 Program", 2 chief scientists of major scientific research programs, 163 chief scientists of national key research and development programs (including 48 international cooperation scientists), 13 national teaching masters, and 43 selected candidates of the National Hundred Million Talents Project. Among the faculty members, there are 19 academicians, 15 chief scientists of the "973 Program", 2 chief scientists of major scientific research programs, 163 chief scientists of national key R&D programs (including 48 international collaborators), 13 national teaching masters, and 43 selected candidates of the National Hundred Million Talents Project. There are 11 innovative research groups of the National Natural Science Foundation of China and 19 innovation teams of the Ministry of Education.

The university implements the educational ideology of building a university of students, scholars and academics, adheres to the philosophy of "education for people, innovation for the soul, responsibility for action", insists on the construction goal of "first-class teaching and first-class undergraduate "We have taken various measures to deepen the reform of education and teaching, promote quality education, and build and improve a dynamic system for cultivating innovative talents. Over the past few decades, more than 700,000 graduates have gone into society, spreading across all walks of life around the world.

In accordance with the strategy of "leading application, basic breakthrough and coordinated development", the university has built up a science and technology innovation system covering three levels: basic research, high-tech research and technology development. It also has four national key laboratories, two national technology innovation centres, one national innovation platform for the integration of education and industry, seven national engineering (technology) research centres, one national clinical medical research centre, one national medical centre, and two national specialised research centres. Medical Center, 2 national specialized laboratories, 1 joint laboratory of "One Belt, One Road", 6 national bases for international scientific and technological cooperation of the Ministry of Science and Technology, and a number of provincial and ministerial scientific research bases.

The university adheres to the idea of "support through service and development through contribution", combines learning, research and production, establishes close cooperation with local governments and leading enterprises in the industry, carries out university-university-enterprise cooperation in science and technology through the establishment of foreign research institutes and technical research centres, and vigorously promotes the transformation of scientific and technological achievements to the ground and serves the regional economic construction. The university will also be able to serve the regional economy.
